TWICE

TWICE is the group of 9 members, NAYEON, JEONGYEON, MOMO, SANA, JIHYO, MINA, DAHYUN, CHAEYOUNG, and TZUYU, gathered together to show bright and healthy energy, and from the moment of debut in the industry with 'Like OOH-AHH' all the way to ‘CHEER UP’, ‘TT’, 'KNOCK KNOCK’, ‘SIGNAL’, 'LIKEY', and 'Heart Shaker', and they are making dazzling outcome with all of the actively played songs on the hit list. Including the legend of re-climbing the chart, all songs taking top of the chart, number 1 in the music broadcasting programs, grand prizes in the year-end award ceremonies, 7 consecutive YouTube 100 million views for the first time as a girl group, TWICE is having glorious days with unlimited love of the fans. TWICE, boasting firm standpoint as a girl group for everyone, now sings love in sweet imagination through the new album, 'What is Love?'.

MORE & MORE

Twice will share their positive and healthy energy in maximized levels through their new album even only with one look and one listen. (직역하면 “TWICE will impress audiences by eyes once and by ears once more through the new album that maximizes positive and healthy energy.”) The comeback happens nine months after “Feel Special”, the previous song, and it is the first new song released in 2020. It will quench the thirst of music fans.

“MORE & MORE”, the title song, sings the emotion of craving the lover when the excitement and sweetness of love matures. The tropical house rhythm of it doubles the trendiness of this song. The refrain of the song has the sound of the instrument, which will help audiences focus on the splendid performance.

WHAT IS LOVE?

The 5th Mini Album 'What is Love?' contains the fluttering heart full of emotion of the 9 members who are curious about love. The title song, 'What is Love?', is a dance song of curiosity and imagination of the girls who learned love through books, movies, or dramas, and it became a topic that the leader of JYP, J.Y. Park ”The Asiansoul” wrote and composed the song. The up-tempo dance beat and the trap-genre rhythm are added to the bright bouncing melody to present musical variations in one song. In the lyrics, there are cute imaginations and curiosities of the girls toward love, such as "Every day, in a movie in a book or in a drama, I feel love. Um- I learn about love", "I wanna know how it could be as sweet as candy", “What is Love? Will love come to me someday".

EYES WIDE OPEN

TWICE will release their 2nd full-length album – “Eyes Wide Open” - on October 26th. It is the second full-length album of TWICE after releasing “Twicetagram”, the first full-length album, on Oct 30, 2017. It reveals the charm of TWICE more boldly. TWICE has challenged various genres of music covering dance-pop (overflowing with grooves), city pop, R&B, and Latin pop through “Eyes Wide Open”. While they sang, the feeling of yearning for love grew more as they fall deeper in love in “More & More”, the previous song, which they put newly recognized feelings in the music this time. The title track, “I Can’t Stop Me”, sings of a conflicting heart on the edge of good and evil and an uncontrollable mind. It falls in the synthwave genre, which is the mix of European electronic sound and American 80s synth sound and features an addictive melody.

Twice presents a more powerful and splendid performance in the new song, “I Can’t Stop Me”. The choreography was completed by the cooperation of Kiel Tutin, who collaborated with Twice for “More & More”, and Jonte Moaning, Beyonce's choreographer. The story of its music video is connected to that of More & More’s music video. It boasts a wealth of attractions with fantastic backgrounds such as gorges and train stations. It is also worth mentioning the sensational and colorful graphic effects, including the nine members appearing in the flowers.

CRY FOR ME

“CRY FOR ME” sings about the confusion in which love and hate are entangled, and you can feel the even deeper and stronger charm of TWICE. The lyrics are written by Jin-young Park, JYP Entertainment's head and a representative K-pop producer, and Heize, a popular singer-songwriter. It depicts the moment when the extremes of both emotions coexist.

Moreover, it is composed by Ryan Tedder, a member of the famous emo rock band “OneRepublic”, Melanie Joy Fontana, a global hit song maker, and Michelle 'Lindgren' Schulz, a renowned producer. TWICE will reveal a black-hole-like charm with this new song “CRY FOR ME” that has captured the public’s attention before its official release.
